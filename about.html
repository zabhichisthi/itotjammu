About Us

Government Industrial Training Institute of Trainers (ITOT), Jammu is a premier government institution dedicated to imparting quality technical education and instructor training.

The institute plays a vital role in strengthening the skill development ecosystem by preparing competent, skilled, and industry-ready instructors through modern infrastructure and experienced faculty.

ITOT Jammu is committed to excellence, discipline, and innovation in vocational education and contributes significantly to nation building through skill development initiatives.
