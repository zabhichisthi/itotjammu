Welcome to Government IToT Jammu

Government Industrial Training Institute of Trainers (ITOT), Jammu is a premier technical institute dedicated to excellence in instructor training and skill development. The institute plays a vital role in strengthening vocational education by providing high-quality, industry-oriented training aligned with national skill initiatives. At ITOT Jammu, we focus on developing competent, disciplined, and job-ready professionals through a blend of theoretical knowledge and extensive hands-on practical training. Our modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry-linked curriculum ensure that trainees are well prepared to meet the evolving demands of the technical education and skilled workforce ecosystem.
