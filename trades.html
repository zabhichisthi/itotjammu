Trades Offered

CITS – Computer Software Application (CSA)

This trade focuses on advanced computer applications, programming fundamentals, and instructional methodologies for IT instructors.

  • Duration: 1 Year
  • Eligibility: Relevant ITI / Diploma
  • Mode: Regular

CITS – Electrician

Provides in-depth knowledge of electrical systems, wiring practices, safety standards, and teaching techniques.

  • Duration: 1 Year
  • Eligibility: ITI Electrician
  • Mode: Regular

CITS – Fitter

Covers mechanical fitting, measurement tools, workshop practices, and instructor-level training.

  • Duration: 1 Year
  • Eligibility: ITI Fitter
  • Mode: Regular

CITS – Electronics Mechanic

Focuses on electronic circuits, devices, troubleshooting, and vocational teaching methods.

  • Duration: 1 Year
  • Eligibility: ITI Electronics
  • Mode: Regular
